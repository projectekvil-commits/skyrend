Choose Your Path

When the sky shattered and the gods vanished, the races of Aden were left to forge their own destiny. Four factions now vie for supremacy.

Humans

Dominion

With the gods silent, humanity refused to kneel. United under the banner of Dominion, they forge their destiny through steel, faith, and unyielding ambition.

Dwarves

Accord

Masters of craft and commerce, the Dwarves banded together under the Accord — a pact sworn in molten iron. What they lack in stature, they forge in firepower.

Elves & Dark Elves

Veil

Once divided by light and shadow, the Elves united behind the Veil when their patron gods fell silent. Ancient magic and moonlit blades now serve a single purpose — survival through unity.

Orcs

Covenant

The Covenant needs no gods. Bound by blood oaths and primal fury, the Orcs saw the Skyrend as liberation. No chains. No masters. Only the strongest will rule the shattered world.
